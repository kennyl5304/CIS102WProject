Part of EarthShield's mission is to spread awareness about the issues of global warming. Listed down below is a table of resources for anyone willing to continue looking into this issue. Remember, global warming is an issue that concerns EVERYONE! There's no way to fix our problems without an Earth for those problems to exist in the first place!


  • Global Climate Change

    An official site created by NASA to document the current statistics concerning global warming. Here, you will find news articles, information, statistics, and everything there is to know about climate change.

  • United Nations

    A subcategory in the offical United Nations website filled with insightful news articles about climate change. This is a helpful site for keeping up to date with the latest news and events.

  • EPA

    EPA, or the Environmental Protection Agency, is a government organization dedicated to helping to preserve the environment. There are also helpful links to other articles on the site where you can look up how to help as well as causes and effects.

  • Natural Resources Defense Council

    A nonprofit organization dedicated to protecting the Earth from issues such as global warming, here you will find helpful FAQs and QnAs about the planet and global warming.
